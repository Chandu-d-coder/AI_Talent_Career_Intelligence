# Dataset Assumptions

## General Assumptions

1. Dataset Is Synthetic Or Simulated.
2. Dataset Is Intended For Workforce Intelligence Analysis.
3. Salary Values Represent Annual Compensation.
4. Bonus Values Represent Annual Bonus Compensation.
5. Job Openings Represent Market Demand.
6. Experience Years Represent Total Professional Experience.
7. Company Ratings Follow A Standardized Rating Scale.
8. Employee Satisfaction Is Measured On A Consistent Scale.
9. Work Life Balance Scores Are Comparable Across Countries.
10. Cost Of Living Index Represents Relative Living Costs.
11. Career Growth Score Represents Long-Term Career Potential.
12. Promotion Speed Represents Relative Promotion Frequency.

## Project Assumptions

1. Dataset Quality Is Sufficient For Analysis.
2. No Additional Data Cleaning Is Required For Missing Values.
3. Duplicate Records Do Not Exist.
4. Workforce Intelligence Modules Can Be Built Directly From Existing Features.
